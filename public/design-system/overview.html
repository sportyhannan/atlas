07 · Design rules at a glance

the non-negotiables
01

Clinical green, always

#0E7A4B is the only accent. No blue-to-purple gradients, ever.

02

No emoji in product

This is a clinical instrument. Status is a colored dot, not an icon face.

03

Every claim cites

NCT, PubMed, Form 1572 — rendered in IBM Plex Mono, linked inline.

04

Dense is a virtue

32 px default row height. 14 px default body. Users want more on screen.

05

Restrained motion

140 ms standard ease. No bounce. No scroll-triggered animation.

06

Sentence case

"Run search" not "Run Search." ALL CAPS only on eyebrow labels.

07

Numerals ≥ 2

"22 sites, 8 countries" beats "twenty-two sites." Always tabular figures.

08

No AI-generated imagery

Atlas's credibility comes from real evidence. Fake hero illustrations undermine it.

09

Max radius 12 px

Inputs 4, cards 8, modals 12. Full radius reserved for pills.
